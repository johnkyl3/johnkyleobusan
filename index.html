Title: Game Guide: "No Skipping, No Turning Back"

Introduction: Welcome to the game "No Skipping, No Turning Back." In this unique and challenging game, you'll face a series of questions. Once you choose an answer, there's no turning back, and the answers will be disabled. Your goal is to navigate through all the questions and reach the end.
  1. Starting the Game: You will immediately be presented with the first question.
  2. Answer Selection: Read the question carefully and consider your options. Choose the answer you believe is correct.
  3. Disabling Answers: Once you select an answer, it will be locked, and you won't be able to change it. There is no going back; your decision is final.
  4. Progress: Track your progress through a series of questions. Reach the final question to complete the game.
  5. Strategies: Take your time to read and analyze each question. Don't rush; there's no time limit. Consider all available information and think critically.
  6. Restarting the Game: If you reach an incorrect answer or want to start over, select "Play" from the main menu.
  7. Perseverance: This game can be challenging, but perseverance is key. Keep playing, learn from your mistakes, and improve your knowledge and strategy.
